About

Jollibee is a Filipino brand that is well loved by the Filipino people. It is a largest fast food chain that has dominated the Philippines. Jollibee is known for its delicious and affordable menu like Chicken Joy, Jolly Spaghetti, and Yumburger. Jollibee was founded in the year 1975 by Dr. Tony Tan Caktong, which started as a small family business that sells ice-creams. Now Jollibee has expanded outside the country.
